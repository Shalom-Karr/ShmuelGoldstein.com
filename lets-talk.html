What to Expect

A conversation, not a pitch.

A few things people ask before booking.

Do I need to prepare?
No. Bring what's on your mind. If we work together later, we'll structure the work then — the Discovery Call is just for talking.
What does it cost?
The Discovery Call is free. If we decide to work together, we'll discuss pricing and structure that fits your situation.
What if we're not a fit?
That's a normal outcome. You'll leave with a clearer picture of what you actually need — even if it's someone else.
Do you work internationally?
Yes. Most sessions are by Zoom or phone. Clients are based around the world.
